<p>Cat Pham is a psychology student and research assistant at RMIT University
Vietnam, as well as a mental health advocate committed to reimagining public
mental health through inclusive, community-driven innovation. She serves
on the Vietnamese Youth Advisory Council for the Youth Promotion of Resilience
Involving Mental e-Health (Y-PRIME) study, where she contributes to the
co-design of a wellbeing app for high school students. Alongside her work
in youth mental health, Cat is passionate about improving access to care
for older adults in Vietnam. Her current interests focus on how digital
tools and intergenerational dialogue can bridge mental health gaps across
the lifespan. She is particularly drawn to culturally responsive approaches
that promote connection between age groups and support sustainable models
of care.</p>
