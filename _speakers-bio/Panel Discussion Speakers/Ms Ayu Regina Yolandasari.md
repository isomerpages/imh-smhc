<p>Ayu Regina Yolandasari is a specialist and advocate for mental health,
gender, and sexuality issues in Indonesia, with more than 10 years of working
experience. She began her career as a Sexual and Gender-Based Violence
(SGBV) counsellor in a Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) and is currently
working independently as a speaker, facilitator, researcher, and mental
health counsellor at Ad Familia Counselling Centre in Jakarta. She is a
member of the International Lived Experience Advisory Panel (I-LEAP), which
supports Research for Mental Health Equity in the Asia Pacific â€“ Digital
(REMAP-D). Additionally, she is an external member of the LGBTIQ+ Sub-Committee
of the Global Mental Health Peer Network (GMHPN), and a coordinator of
the coalition against sexual violence civil society in Indonesia.</p>
