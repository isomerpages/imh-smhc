<p>A pharmacist by training with over 30 years of experience, Dr Tan Weng
Mooi leads in the development and implementation of Healthy Precinct Framework
(HPF) and digital solutions to activate community for health. She also
co-chairs the Digital Mental Health Implementation Committee (DMHIC) with
the National Mental Health Office of MOH (NMHO) which aims to develop digital
mental health plans and initiatives. Weng Mooi is committed in transforming
health and community care to be person-centric, empowering, holistic and
nature-integrated particularly in mental health and dementia care.</p>
