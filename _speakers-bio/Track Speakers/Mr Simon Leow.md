<p>Simon Leow is the co-founder of Happiness Initiative, a social enterprise
dedicated to fostering well-being across Singapore and the region. Passionate
about making well-being skills, tools, and initiatives accessible to everyone,
Simon oversees the research and development<strong> </strong>arm at Happiness
Initiative. He loves a data-driven approach to building a culture of well-being
within organisations and communities. Simon’s strengths include creativity,
love for learning, and gratitude. His research interests lie at the intersection
of well-being and travel. Simon holds a Master’s in Applied Positive Psychology
from the University of Pennsylvania. A fun fact about him is that he loves
to live off grid in a tiny house with his dog Ricco. He loves nature, camping,
van life, and outdoor cooking.&nbsp;</p>
