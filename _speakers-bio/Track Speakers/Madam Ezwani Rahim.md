<p>Madam Ezwani Rahim is a volunteer Caregiver Advocate with Club Heal, where
she actively supports and uplifts the caregiver community through meaningful,
engaging activities and initiatives.
<br>In her professional capacity, she serves as a part-time lecturer and mentor,
guiding diploma students through their practicum and academic development.
<br>Her passion lies in empowering caregivers and nurturing the next generation
of professionals, sharing insights that are both refreshing and impactful.
<br>In her session, Madam Ezwani will share a range of refreshing and purposeful
activities designed by Club Heal that have positively supported caregivers
and anyone caring for their loved ones, offering both inspirational and
practical insights for sustaining emotional and mental well-being.</p>
