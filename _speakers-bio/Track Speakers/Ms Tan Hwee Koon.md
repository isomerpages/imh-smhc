<p>Ms. Hwee Koon TAN is a research specialist at the Translational Research
Division of the National Council of Social Services (NCSS), where she researches
quality of life across different population segments, identifying emerging
needs and trends. Her research interests are in early years development
and children’s quality of life, including understanding the well-being
of children with developmental delays and disabilities. Currently on secondment
to NCSS from the Ministry of Digital Development and Information (MDDI),
Hwee Koon previously was in MDDI’s Research and Data Division where her
area of work was in analysing economic trends and industry sentiments.</p>
