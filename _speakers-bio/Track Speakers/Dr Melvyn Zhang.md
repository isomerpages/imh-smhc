<p>Dr Melvyn Zhang is a Senior Consultant at the National Addictions Management
Service. He is also an Assistant Professor with the Lee Kong Chian School
of Medicine. He has a special interest in treating adolescents with behavioural
addictions, in particular, that of gaming disorder. In 2024, he has led
a team of authors in publishing&nbsp;a Guide for Gaming disorders for the
helping professionals, to increase their awareness of the association between
gaming disorders and other mental health disorders. He has extensive research
publications, focusing on several research areas, which include that of
addictive disorders, the interface of psychiatric disorder and medical
disorders, and E and M Health innovations.</p>
