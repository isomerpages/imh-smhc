<p>Dr Chang obtained her Master of Medicine (Family Medicine) in 2014 and
accreditation as a Family Physician in 2015. She also has a Graduate Diploma
in Family Practice Dermatology and a Graduate Diploma in Geriatric medicine.
<br>She continued to develop herself professionally and was admitted as a
Fellow of the College of Family Physicians (FCFP) of Singapore in 2021.
<br>Dr Chang worked for 12 years in NHG polyclinics and was the Deputy Head
of Ang Mo Kio Polyclinic from 2019 to 2023. She is also the Deputy advisor
for the Women’s Specialty Advisory Group and was part of the Cervical Screening
Singapore Implementation workgroup for HPV-DNA Primary testing.
<br>She believes in holistic care, health promotion and preventive care for
patients from birth to their senior years. She aims to understand patients
through their values and life course to provide quality and comprehensive
care. Other than managing acute and chronic conditions, she is also adept
at minor surgical procedures. Besides doing clinical work, Dr Chang is
also an adjunct clinical teacher at Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine.</p>
