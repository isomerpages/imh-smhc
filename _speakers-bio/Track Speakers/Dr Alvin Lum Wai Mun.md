<p>Dr Alvin Lum started the IMH Mental Health-General Practitioner Partnership
Programme and is the Deputy Director of the programme. It aims to right-site
stable patients with mental illness to the primary care setting, within
a community of trained GPs who are psychologically-minded and effectively
manage these patients in the community. He is also the Deputy Head of Medical
Services at IMH. Since 2012, Dr Lum is part of the teaching faculty and
Lecturer of the Graduate Diploma in Mental Health. Dr Lum is also an Adjunct
Lecturer in the Department of FM, Yong Loo Ling School of Medicine.
<br>Dr Lum has over 20 years of experience in primary care and was a Family
Physician and GP in private practice. Dr Lum also has a strong interest
in research, involved with the Early Psychosis Intervention Programme,
which led to a scholarship when he was appointed a Visiting Research Fellow
at the Research Department at IMH.</p>
