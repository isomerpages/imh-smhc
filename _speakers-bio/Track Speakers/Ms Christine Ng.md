<p>Christine has been with the Health Promotion Board for five years, serving
as the campaign lead for mental health initiatives. She develops and implements
impactful mental health awareness campaigns that have earned multiple industry
awards. Christine's work focuses on breaking stigma, encouraging open conversations
about mental health, and empowering individuals to take charge of their
well-being. Her expertise lies in crafting purpose-driven marketing strategies
that effectively reach and engage diverse audiences across Singapore. Through
her work, Christine continues to explore innovative ways to use purpose-driven
marketing in promoting mental health awareness and support in Singapore.</p>
