<p>James Tan is the Chief Executive Officer of TOUCH Community Services,
a multi-service non-profit organisation that serves people of all ages,
races and religions to see sustainable change and transformation in their
lives.
<br>With more than three decades of transformative service, James has continually
positioned TOUCH to be forward-thinking in addressing evolving needs in
the community.&nbsp; In recognition of good governance practices, TOUCH
was awarded the Charity Governance Award 2023 and Charity Transparency
Awards by the Charity Council. The organisation’s commitment to developing
human capital also won it the Special Recognition Award (Social Service
Sector) in the Kincentric Best Employers Award 2021.&nbsp;
<br>Beyond his current role at TOUCH, James believes in contributing back
to the sector. He is currently serving on several interagency taskforces
including the Agency for Integrated Care’s Community Care Manpower Committee
and Beyond the Label Collective, a mental health anti-stigma national movement
amongst other appointments. &nbsp;</p>
