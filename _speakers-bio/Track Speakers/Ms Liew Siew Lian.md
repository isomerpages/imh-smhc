<p>Ms Liew Siew Lian has dedicated 23 years of service to Singapore's Ministry
of Education, transitioning from her initial role as a teacher to become
a school counsellor. She holds a Master of Counselling degree and is a
certified Master Solution-Focused Practitioner, accredited by the International
Alliance of Solution-Focused Teaching Institutes. Her commitment to student
care earned her the National Day Efficiency Medal. With experience spanning
primary, secondary and junior college levels, Siew Lian has gained valuable
insights into students' developmental needs across different stages. She
is passionate about using strength-based approaches in school counselling,
emphasising students' resilience and capabilities. Central to her practice
is the belief in fostering strong partnerships with educators and parents
to establish comprehensive support systems for students.</p>
