<p>Ms Jamie Soo is the Lead Social Worker at Montfort Care, with 24 years
of experience in social services. She has worked extensively with individuals
facing mental health challenges, particularly the elderly, as well as families
and children, shelter residents, ex-offenders, and those with disabilities.
Jamie is dedicated to a person-centred care approach, focusing on the unique
needs of each individual. Her work has made a significant impact, especially
for those with mental health issues, by offering tailored support that
helps clients feel heard, valued, and empowered. Through her compassionate
approach, Jamie helps individuals build confidence, develop coping strategies,
and improve their overall well-being. She works closely with clients to
overcome barriers, gain independence, and enhance their quality of life.
Jamie’s efforts have created lasting change, helping vulnerable individuals
lead healthier, more fulfilling lives and navigate challenges with resilience.</p>
