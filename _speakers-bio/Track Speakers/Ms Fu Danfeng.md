<p>Fu Danfeng is currently a senior counsellor at Samaritans of Singapore
Limited (SOS). Since 2017, she has been working alongside suicide loss
survivors both in individual counselling and support groups. She is also
part of the Local Outreach to Suicide Survivors (LOSS) team, which oversees
a full range of support services at SOS for those who have lost loved ones
to suicide. Danfeng has also been involved in planning the curriculum for
Healing Within - a structured support group programme conducted by SOS,
which started its first run in 2021 for suicide loss survivors. In addition,
she collaborates closely with survivor volunteers to support other survivors
in facilitating support groups, curating workshops and engaging in advocacy
work.&nbsp;</p>
