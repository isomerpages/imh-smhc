<p>Dr Janhavi Ajit Vaingankar has over 25 years of research experience ranging
from experimental to epidemiology and public health research. She is a
co-investigator for large national surveys, including the National Youth
Mental Health Study, the Singapore Mental Health Study, and the Wellbeing
of Singapore Elderly study. These studies have serially assessed the prevalence
and determinants of mental health conditions, caregiving impacts, health
literacy, and treatment needs in Singapore’s population. Her research also
covers positive mental health and mental well-being, specifically among
multi-ethnic Asian populations and youths.&nbsp;She has over 200 publications
arising from her research.</p>
