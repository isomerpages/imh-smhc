<p>A/P Jungup Lee is an Associate Professor in the Department of Social Work
and Co-Director of Social Service Research Centre at National University
of Singapore. Her research primarily focuses on youth mental health and
digital well-being, cyberbullying and online sexual harms, and technology
in children and youth development. &nbsp;She currently conducts various
research projects under her research interests, such as an exploratory
study on the mechanism of cyberbullying in a multipronged approach, a mixed-method
study (surveys, interviews, and text mining) of campus sexual misconduct
and online harassment, human-AI cooperation on mental illness stigma, and
an interdisciplinary cross-cultural study on the integration of technology
innovation on youth mental health and wellness. &nbsp;</p>
