<p>Ms. Bettina Yeap is Principal Counsellor and the founder of INSIGHT, the
youth mental health team at Care Corner Singapore Ltd. Holding a B.A. in
Psychology and Sociology and an M.A. in Counselling, Bettina is passionate
about helping individuals navigate life’s challenges. Her work includes
mental wellbeing outreach, clinical counselling for individuals, families,
and couples, as well as supervision and group therapy. She also trains
others in counselling skills and leads talks and workshops on family life
and mental health. Bettina has contributed to workgroups for Beyond the
Label, the Implementation Committee for the Tiered Care Model (Adult),
and the Working Committee for the Tiered Care Model (Youth). She is currently
co-leading a digital mental health workgroup under MOHT.</p>
