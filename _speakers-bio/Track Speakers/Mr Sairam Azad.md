<p>Mr Sairam currently serves as Deputy Director at Caregiving &amp; Community
Mental Health Division of AIC and oversees implementation of the CREST
&amp; COMIT programmes in the community.
<br>Sairam has vast experience in managing Healthcare Operations and successfully
ran a range of eldercare services across different settings - centre-based,
home-based, residential, and community-based. A skilled physiotherapist
by training with over 15 years of experience, Sairam is passionate about
enabling access to mental health services in the community for the residents
and has personal affinity for care integration and seamless care transitions
to support place-based care.
<br>Sairam made significant contributions to the community care and Intermediate
and Long-Term Care sectors through active participation in various committees
and workgroups that were tasked to drive sector-level initiatives.
<br>Sairam holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Physiotherapy, a Master of
Business Administration in Healthcare Management and a post-graduate diploma
in Developmental Rehabilitation.</p>
