<p>Ainul has been with the Health Promotion Board for two decades, amassing
a wealth of experience in health promotion. Her expertise includes programme
development and curating effective outreach strategies to promote initiatives
to youths and hard-to-reach target segments. Currently, Ainul oversees
programmes and campaigns focused on dementia education and mental well-being
for seniors and in the workplace. She also plays a key role in developing
and promoting mental health initiatives for ethnic minority communities.
Ainul holds a degree in Psychology from the University of Wollongong and
a Graduate Diploma in Life Coaching from the Singapore University of Social
Sciences.
